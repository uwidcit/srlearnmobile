CSRP

The CARDI Small Ruminant Project is a collaborative initiative between CARDI, UWI, and various stakeholders within the small ruminant industry within the Caribbean. The project aims at helping farmers in the sheep and goat industry to increase productivity through the use of best practice husbandry and enterprise management techniques. Source

CARDI

The CARDI Small Ruminant Project is a collaborative initiative between various stakeholders within the Caribbean's small ruminant industry. The project will help farmers in the sheep and goat industry to increase productivity through improving husbandry and management techniques. Source

AgriNeTT

AgriNeTT is a project of the St. Augustine Campus of the University of the West Indies that aims to provide ICT-based solutions within the Agricultural sector of the Caribbean. Source
